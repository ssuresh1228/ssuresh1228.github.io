CSC-196w Assignment 2a

My name is Sai Suresh, and I'm really interested in GPUs and PC hardware in general, as well as building and modifying mechanical keyboards.

The image below is the NovelKeys NK65, which was my first custom keyboard. In this build, I used Minterly's Alpaca switches which are linear switches with a fairly heavy 78 gram spring weight. For keycaps, I went with the Akko Neons in the ASA profile which I chose because this profile is essentially a shorter SA profile. For modding, I used Tempest's tape mod which is just applying a couple layers of painter's tape on the back of the PCB, and added switch films to all of the switches while lubing them (takes a really long time to do). I also used a fake gasket mount (burger mount) for the plate which basically consists of mounting all the hardware on small gaskets.
